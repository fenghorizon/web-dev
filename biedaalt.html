Why Fixed

The Philosophy

Fixed gear riding is not a trend. It is a discipline. When you strip away the gears, the cables, the complexity, you are left with something pure — just you, your legs, and the road.

Every VELOX bike is designed with a single principle: nothing that does not need to be there. Light. Responsive. Honest.

We source the finest chromoly and carbon, build by hand, and ship to riders who understand that the best feeling in cycling is a perfectly balanced fixed gear on a clear stretch of tarmac.
