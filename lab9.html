⚙️ Game Setup

📖 Game Rules

  • 4 Knuckle Bones: Each has 4 faces - Horse (морь), Camel (төмөр), Sheep (хонь), Goat (ямаа)
  • Count Horses: Count how many bones land as Horse (морь)
  • Movement: Move your horse forward by the number of horses rolled (1-4)
  • Special Roll: All 4 types (дөрвөн бэрх) = move 4 spaces!
  • No Horses: If no bones land as Horse, skip your turn
  • Victory: First horse to reach the finish line wins!
